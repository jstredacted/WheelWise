    Introduction

    The dataset contains used-car sales transactions with fields for vehicle details, seller, condition, odometer reading, market reference value (MMR), final selling price, and sale date.

    The data likely originates from an operational automotive sales platform where each transaction captures a specific sale event.

    This report converts raw operational records into analytical summaries by category, time, and pricing behavior.

    OLTP Perspective

    Why this is transactional data

    One row represents one completed car sale transaction. It stores what car was sold, by whom, when, and for how much.

    Operational characteristics

    The data is event-level and high-volume, designed for day-to-day operational recording rather than summarized reporting.

    Likely source system

    A vehicle sales transaction management system integrated with dealer inventory and auction workflows.
